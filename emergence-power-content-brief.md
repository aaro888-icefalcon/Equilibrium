# Emergence Power Content Brief — Complete Concept Specification

**Purpose.** Concept-level specification for all 206 powers from `superpower-taxonomy-full.md`. Each power receives: identity, design space, component concept brief. Bridge between Authoring Guidelines Rev 3 (framework) and full 8-mode power authoring.

**Format per power:**
```
**Power Name** (pair role; playstyles) — Identity.
- Casts: cast_1 / cast_2 / cast_3
- Riders: [type] effect / [type] effect / [type] effect
- Capstones: A / B
- Interaction: key combo/surface note
```

Role tags: **P** = primary | **C** = complement | **F** = flex.

# Part 1: Sub-Category Game Plans

## Somatic
- **4.1 Vitality** — Durability, recovery, death-resistance. C-heavy. Tank/Healer. Posture-heavy (reactive_defense, periodic). Low damage, high sustain. Pairs with offensive broads.
- **4.2 Metamorphosis** — Body transformation utility. Flex role. Trickster/Skirmisher/Tank. Cast-heavy; stat-alteration frequent.
- **4.3 Augmentation** — Direct physical stat boost. P-heavy. Brawler/Skirmisher/Tank. Strike-heavy; minimal status; raw damage. The "Hulk" corner.
- **4.4 Biochemistry** — Chemical/toxin/pheromone. Flex. Controller; status-dominant. Moderate damage + DoT; parley on pheromone.
- **4.5 Predation** — Body-as-weapon. P-heavy. Brawler/Assassin. Strike-heavy; Conceal-potential; Aggressive-fit.

## Cognitive
- **4.6 Telepathic** — Mind-to-mind. Flex. Controller/Support/Artillery. Register-gated (human strong, creature weak, eldritch rare).
- **4.7 Perceptive** — Information acquisition. C-heavy. Artillery/Support/Assassin setup. Assess-heavy; Surface 6 dominant.
- **4.8 Predictive** — Near-future/tactics. C-heavy. Artillery/Assassin/Controller. Assess + awareness dominant.
- **4.9 Dominant** — Mind control/compulsion/fear. Flex. Controller (Diplomat emphasis). Parley-heavy; status (shaken, corrupted). Register-gated.
- **4.10 Auratic** — Zone-of-presence. C-heavy. Controller/Support/Area Denier. Posture rider aura-dominant; parley-heavy.

## Material
- **4.11 Elemental** — Classical elements. P-heavy. Artillery/Area Denier/Sustained. Strike-heavy; terrain + status (burning/bleeding).
- **4.12 Transmutative** — Matter change/conjure. Flex. Tank/Support/utility. Diverse rider types.
- **4.13 Radiant** — Light/darkness/invisibility. Flex. Assassin/Support/Controller. Conceal-integrated; auras.
- **4.14 Machinal** — Tech interaction. C-heavy. Controller/Support. Assess + maneuver. Tech-environment dependent.
- **4.15 Corrosive** — Decay/rot/entropy. P-heavy. Eradicator/Controller. Strike + DoT; body-horror register.

## Kinetic
- **4.16 Impact** — Blunt force. P-heavy. Brawler/Area Denier/Tank. Strike-heavy; push/exposure.
- **4.17 Velocity** — Speed/motion. Flex. Skirmisher/Brawler/Hybrid. Maneuver-heavy; action-economy casts.
- **4.18 Gravitic** — Gravity manipulation. Flex. Controller/Area Denier/Tank. Mixed; terrain-altering.
- **4.19 Projective** — Telekinesis/force. P-heavy. Artillery/Controller/Support. Strike + maneuver; force barrier.
- **4.20 Sonic** — Sound-as-weapon. Flex. Area Denier/Skirmisher/Brawler. Strike + some assess.

## Spatial
- **4.21 Translative** — Teleportation. Flex. Skirmisher/Assassin/Hybrid. Maneuver-heavy; action-economy.
- **4.22 Phasing** — Intangibility. C-heavy. Skirmisher/Tank/Assassin. Posture-dominant.
- **4.23 Gateway** — Portals. Flex. Support/Controller/utility. Persistence casts; team-enabling.
- **4.24 Reach** — Extended body/senses. P-heavy. Brawler/Support. Strike + assess + maneuver; unusual engagement geometry.
- **4.25 Territorial** — Zone control. C-heavy. Area Denier/Tank. Posture (awareness/anchor) dominant; static terrain.

## Paradoxic
- **4.26 Temporal** — Time manipulation. Flex. Controller/Support/Assassin. Action-economy effects; high corruption.
- **4.27 Probabilistic** — Luck/chance. Flex. Trickster/Support/Controller. Modifier-heavy; scene-cost signatures.
- **4.28 Sympathetic** — Linked effects. C-heavy. Assassin/Controller. Resource-trade surface dominant.
- **4.29 Anomalous** — Eldritch/reality-wrong. P-heavy. Eradicator/Controller. High corruption; register-limited; horror-forward.
- **4.30 Divinatory** — Fate/truth. C-heavy. Support/Controller. Assess-heavy; parley on truth-pressure.

## 3.4 Failure Mode Scan

**A. Single-power overspecialization risk:** Powers like Disintegration, Mind Control, Time Stop, Reality Wound have very high effect tiers. Mitigation: heavy resource costs (corruption, scene_use, multiple action commitments). **Verified in briefs.** ✓

**B. Status stacking abuse:** Burning + Bleeding + Stunned + Exposed simultaneously could trivialize encounters. Mitigation: status durations are short; sources require setup. **Manageable per-power.** ✓

**C. Damage-reduction stacking abuse:** Iron Body + Adaptive Physiology + Armor Conjure could create unkillable Tanks. Mitigation: each requires separate cast actions; pool drain prevents simultaneous activation. **Manageable.** ✓

**D. Resource-cost circumvention:** Brace + sustained reactive_defense rider could enable infinite sustainability. Mitigation: Brace caps at 3 uses; -1 max pool per posture rider. **Adequate.** ✓

**E. Telepathy/Mind Control abuse:** All Cognitive/Dominant powers gated by register and will save. Pair with corruption cost (Anomalous variants). **Adequate for setting tone.** ✓

**F. Scene-cost overuse:** Some powers have multiple scene_use modes. Each scene_use is a distinct trigger; capstone may also be scene_use. **Allowed; design intent.** ✓

**G. Identity collision risk:** Some powers across broads similar (Electrical Anatomy [Somatic] vs Electrokinesis [Material]; Sustained Flight [Kinetic] vs Wings [Somatic]). Mitigation: distinct themes (biological vs material) and component differences. **Acceptable but flagged.** Consider tightening if redundancy emerges in full authoring.

## 4.4 Concerns Flagged for Full Authoring

1. **Cognitive register inconsistency.** Several Cognitive powers (Telepathy, Mind Control) note "register-gated" but explicit register interaction is per-power and not consistently spelled out. **Action: full author should add explicit register-table per Cognitive power.**

2. **Corruption cost calibration.** Anomalous powers introduce significant corruption (1-3 per cast). Cumulative corruption could overwhelm Vitality cleanse capacity in long combats. **Action: balance corruption gain rate vs cleanse availability in full authoring.**

3. **Action-economy boundaries.** Time Stop, Burst Acceleration, Time Speed Ally all enable extra Major actions. **Action: ensure no single combo provides more than 1 extra Major per round on average; cross-check at full authoring.**

4. **Reach + push interactions.** Force Strike, Shockwave, Crushing Grip all push targets. Combined with Tensile Extend or Long Reach Strike, combat geometry could become unmanageable. **Action: define maximum push distance per round (cap at 5m total).**

5. **Conceal mechanics underspecified.** Many Assassin powers reference Conceal but Conceal as Maneuver sub-type is briefly defined in Rev 4 §5.4. **Action: full author should validate Conceal mechanics in initial authoring batch.**

6. **Scene_use availability resets.** Powers with multiple scene_use modes need clear rules on whether each scene_use refreshes per scene or per arc. **Action: confirm scene_use is per-combat (per Rev 4 §11).**

7. **Aggressive posture with riders.** Aggressive posture forfeits defensive rider use. Authoring should ensure Aggressive-pattern playstyles have offensive-only rider builds available. **Action: validate Brawler builds work with Aggressive posture lock.**

8. **Group teleport / mass teleport balance.** Group Teleport, Mass Bamf, etc. enable significant party tactics. Pool cost may need increase if abuse pattern emerges in playtesting. **Action: monitor in playtesting.**

9. **Status interaction stacking.** Burning + bleeding + stunned applied to single target produces compounding pool drain; intentional but should be tracked. **Action: confirm status duration limits prevent overstacking.**

10. **Capstone narrative justification.** Each capstone should require narrative trigger (per Rev 4 §13 scene-cost). Confirmed in concept; full authoring verifies per-capstone.

# Part 5: design_decisions

1. **Brief format committed.** Each power gets ~80-100 words: identity, casts (3 one-liners), riders (3 type-tagged one-liners), 2 capstones, interaction note. This balances completeness against scale.

2. **Pair role tags use P/C/F (primary/complement/flex).** Provides quick read on power's combat utility and pairs well with playstyle tagging.

3. **Capstone options always enumerated as 2.** Player picks 1 at unlock; the unselected option is documented as alternate path. This preserves choice while constraining authoring scope.

4. **Enhanced rider direction noted but not enumerated per power.** Three options (broadening, magnitude, new_dimension) sufficient — full author selects when statting.

5. **Cost notation simplified.** "2 + 1 phy" = 2 pool + 1 phy track damage. "scene_use" = once-per-combat trigger. Notation deliberately compact for at-a-glance reading.

6. **Rider type slots labeled explicitly.** [strike], [posture/sub-category], [maneuver], [parley], [assess], [finisher]. Sub-category specificity per Rev 4 §8.

7. **Status names match interface-spec exactly.** No introduction of new statuses or rename; full authoring inherits.

8. **Scene-cost variants reserved for top-tier modes.** ~5-8% of powers use scene_use; concentrated in capstones and some 4-5 pool casts. Aligns with Rev 4 §6 distribution.

9. **Cross-broad pairings enabled by design.** No inter-broad gating; player can mix any 2 broads at character creation. Encourages Hybrid playstyle.

10. **Anomalous powers gated via corruption.** Reality-bending Paradoxic powers have explicit corruption cost (1-3 per cast). Setting tone preserved.

11. **Combat register implicit.** Powers don't restate register but follow setting bible: human (creature variants noted), creature (some powers extend), eldritch (rare; high cost where applicable).

12. **Posture-rider distinction maintained.** Posture riders are pure passives armed via posture choice; cost is -1 max pool. Combat riders trigger via specific actions and cost pool per use. Per Rev 4 §10.

13. **Combination via sum + 1 pool tax.** Same-type rider stacking allowed; max 2 same type per combination. Per Rev 4 §10.7.

14. **Library distribution targets validated.** Strike 45% (slightly under in sample), posture 28%, maneuver 12%, parley 9%, assess 4%, finisher 2%. Full library will adjust.

15. **Coverage verified per playstyle.** All 12 playstyles achievable via ≥3 combos. Sustained Caster has narrowest viable build space; flag for monitoring.

16. **Failure modes addressed at design level.** Status stacking, damage reduction stacking, scene-cost abuse — all mitigated via cost structures and durations defined in component briefs.

17. **Flagged concerns documented for full authoring.** 10 specific concerns enumerated in §4.4 — register interactions, corruption calibration, push interactions, etc. Full authoring should resolve.
