## About the Game

Emergence is a solo AI-narrated tactical RPG set one year after the Onset — a catastrophic event that killed most of humanity and left survivors with supernatural abilities.

You make choices. The engine resolves mechanics. The narrator tells you what happens.

**Game modes:**
- **Session Zero**: 10-scene character creation
- **Simulation**: Day-by-day world with situations and choices
- **Combat**: Turn-based tactical encounters with 8 action verbs

**Key concepts:**
- Powers are physical facts, not spectacle
- Violence has cost and consequence
- Relationships, factions, and corruption track your impact
- Your character will eventually die — plan for a descendant

For full game details, see `emergence/PLAY.md`.
