# THE POWER SYSTEM

## The Onset

The Onset occurred at 14:23 UTC on a Thursday in late May. Every operating electronic device in the mid-Atlantic stopped functioning. Simultaneously, approximately 48 million people manifested powers. What caused it is not known.

Within three hours, aircraft in flight had crashed. Traffic signals, subways, elevators, ventilators, refrigeration systems — all stopped. Over 45,000 people in the mid-Atlantic died in the first ninety minutes from transportation-related failures alone. Another 25,000 died in Manhattan from uncontrolled manifestation-accidents during the first three hours. Fifty-five thousand died in subway tunnels during the first twenty-four hours.

Approximately 95% of humans in the mid-Atlantic manifested abilities. Approximately 10% underwent species-level transformation into one of ten metahuman variants. Approximately 1.5% transformed into eldritch entities. The remainder — perhaps 7-10% — died or remained unaltered.

The manifestation wave was regional but not uniform. Primary-cluster regions for specific metahuman species received proportionally more species-transformations. Tier distribution across manifested individuals followed a power-law curve with T1 most common and higher tiers progressively rarer.

The cause is contested. No theory has empirical support. The Onset happened; it has not recurred; what it was remains the central unexplained event of the age.

## Categories

Powers sort into seven descriptive domains. These are organizing labels, not ontological categories. No authority treats the seven as metaphysical truth — the framework emerged empirically from the first two years of observation.

**Physical / Kinetic (~28% primary).** Body, motion, applied force. Kinetic output, enhanced strength and durability, speed, self-propulsion and flight, reflexive redirection of force, localized gravity manipulation, momentum manipulation, structural body reshaping. The broadest and most common domain.

**Perceptual / Mental (~15% primary).** Perception, cognition, communication, influence over thought and emotion. Enhanced senses, extrasensory perception, telepathy, empathy, memory manipulation, suggestion and mind control, illusion, psychic damage, precognition, retrocognition, clairvoyance, power mimicry. The most socially dangerous domain because many manifestations are hidden.

**Matter / Energy (~14% primary).** Manipulation of non-living material — stone, metal, wood, glass — and energy forms including electromagnetic, thermal, chemical. Matter reshaping, thermal control, electromagnetic manipulation, state-change effects, projection of solid or energetic forms.

**Biological (~14% primary).** Living tissue, including healing, agricultural biokinetic, animal influence, self-modification of body. The domain that ended regional famine. Agricultural biokinetic techniques are the reason the mid-Atlantic stopped starving.

**Auratic (~12% primary).** Persistent zones of influence around the user — trust fields, calm fields, dread fields, clarity fields. Invisible in cause, visible in effect. Zones persist in the user's presence and fade when the user leaves, though heavy use marks the location.

**Temporal / Spatial (~9% primary).** Time and space. Short-range teleportation, folded inventory, brief time manipulation, remote viewing, precognition (overlaps with Perceptual), spatial awareness, harbor-scale logistics work. The rarest domain to recruit for because specialists are scarce.

**Eldritch (~1.5% primary as primary domain; overlaps with 15% of anomalous entity profile).** Bargained or pact-derived effects, contact with non-human intelligences, cursed projections, mark-reading. Most primary-Eldritch users are entities rather than humans; human primary-Eldritch users are usually cult-associated.

A manifested person usually has a **profile** — one dominant capacity plus one or two secondaries. Roughly 55% of manifested individuals have capacities in more than one domain. The seven domains are categorical labels for the dominant effect, not underlying mechanism.

## Tier System

Tier is a **holistic** assessment of overall capability, integrated across a person's full profile as they would manifest under pressure. It is not a per-domain measurement.

Tier scaling, for a narrow specialist in a concrete domain (Physical/kinetic used as reference):

- **T1** — visibly above-human. Lift 300kg; run 40 km/h sustained; absorb a baseball bat strike without injury.
- **T3** — impressive. Flip a car; sprint past a motorcycle briefly; skin stops small knives.
- **T5** — superhuman. Throw a car across a lot; sustained flight; walk through small-caliber gunfire.
- **T7** — destructive. Level buildings; kilometer-per-minute flight; stop artillery fire.
- **T9** — apocalyptic. Destroy city blocks; sustain flight indefinitely; near-invulnerability to conventional weapons.
- **T10+** — mythic. No mid-Atlantic individual has reliably achieved T10+.

Multi-domain users often test higher than narrow-depth equivalents because combination is greater than sum. Someone with Physical-T4, Perceptual-T4, and Auratic-T3 capacities may functionally be T6 overall. Tier is the integration, not the arithmetic.

**Tier pyramid (humans, fraction of manifested):** T1 ~40%, T2 ~25%, T3 ~15%, T4 ~10%, T5 ~5%, T6 ~3%, T7 ~1.5%, T8 ~0.4%, T9 ~0.08%, T10+ ~0.005%. In a population of 16M manifested, roughly 8,000 are T5+, roughly 200 are T7+, roughly 10 are T9+.

## Manifestation

Manifestation on Onset Day was binary — you either did or you did not. Approximately 95% of humans did. Profile and starting tier emerged over the subsequent hours and days. Most manifesters did not know what they could do for weeks; many discovered their abilities through accidents or crises.

Post-Onset-born children manifest at puberty, with about 99% rate. Children born to two manifested parents manifest reliably; children with one manifested and one unmanifested parent manifest about 98% of the time; children of two unmanifested parents (rare post-Onset) manifest about 80% of the time. Parental-domain heritability exists but is weak — a kinetic parent is somewhat more likely to have kinetic children, but the effect is not strong and does not override other factors.

Species-level transformation appears to have been a one-time Onset Day effect. Post-Onset-born children of metahuman parents are born with species traits reliably; they do not "transform" subsequently. Cross-species pairings almost always fail to produce viable offspring (~95% failure rate).

## Heredity

Heredity is weak but real. A manifested parent's primary domain is about twice as likely to appear in a manifested child than chance alone would predict. Tier does not inherit strongly — a T7 parent is no more likely to have a T5+ child than any other manifested parent. What appears to inherit more reliably is **profile breadth** — a multi-domain parent tends to have multi-domain children, and a narrow-specialist parent tends to have narrow-specialist children.

This is the observed pattern for the first post-Onset-born cohort reaching puberty around T+7 to T+10. The sample is small and longer-horizon patterns are unknown.

## Growth

Within a given tier, a manifester continues developing for 10–20 years of practice before reaching the upper end of that tier. Most people stop there. Active military or breakthrough-aggressive individuals can reach the upper end faster; unused or passive manifesters may never reach it.

Growth is domain-appropriate: Physical users grow through physical training, Perceptual through attention practice, Biological through case-work, Auratic through sustained presence in contexts that exercise the zone, and so on. A user who does not exercise their capacity plateaus below their tier ceiling.

## Breakthrough

Advancement to the next tier requires a **breakthrough condition** — an acute catalyst that enables the user to cross the tier boundary holistically. Most manifesters never breakthrough past their initial tier; breakthroughs are rare, marked, and load-bearing.

**Three breakthrough types:**

- **Depth** — existing capacities deepen. A T6 kinetic becomes a T7 kinetic. The narrow-specialist route.
- **Breadth** — a new domain appears in the profile. A T6 pure-kinetic develops an Auratic capacity, rising to T7 overall because the profile is now richer.
- **Integration** — existing capacities begin combining in ways they did not before. The classic "fighting style" breakthrough — the parts were always there, but now they work together.

**Observed catalysts** for breakthrough:

- **Life-threat** — mortal danger forcing access to latent capacity. Most common. Produces all three breakthrough types.
- **Sacrifice** — giving up something irrevocable. Usually Depth or Breadth.
- **Mentorship** — sustained contact with a higher-tier user. Often produces Integration.
- **Exposure** — contact with specific phenomena (Onset-adjacent sites, eldritch breach zones, transformed terrain). Can produce any type, including Breadth.
- **Domain-specific crisis** — Physical through sustained overload survival; Biological through saving another at severe personal cost; Eldritch through direct entity contact with consent to bargain; Temporal through surviving a temporal paradox event. These usually produce Depth.
- **Loss** — rarely, users who *lose* a capacity (through injury, corruption) find their remaining capacities re-concentrate and deepen, producing tier rise through loss rather than gain.

Every breakthrough leaves a **mark**. The user is physically, mentally, or metaphysically altered in a way that accumulates. Narrow specialists tend toward concentrated marks (Kostas's mineralization, Preston's loss of fear). Multi-domain users tend toward diverse marks across the profile's scope.

Preston's three breakthroughs in months 1–2 of Year 1 are the archetype of the rapid-depth route: T6 → T7 defending refugees from Wretches; T7 → T8 stopping a landslide; T8 → T9 surviving a Hunter pack confrontation. All three Depth type. The cost was loss of fear. He is the most formidable individual in the mid-Atlantic and he is socially isolated because of what the breakthroughs took.

Breakthrough to T10+ is functionally mythic. No individual in the mid-Atlantic has achieved one. Rumors of T10+ individuals in East Asia, South Asia, and West Africa circulate; these individuals are believed to have highly integrated multi-domain profiles. Narrow-specialist ceiling is thought to be T9–T10.

## Costs

Each domain carries distinct cost patterns. Multi-domain users accumulate costs across domains, though not linearly.

- **Physical** — high caloric cost, fatigue. Corruption: body densification, reduced flexibility. Usually visible (musculature, posture).
- **Perceptual** — cognitive fatigue, headache. Corruption: loss of thought-privacy (others' thoughts bleed in), pseudo-memories. Subtle to invisible.
- **Matter/Energy** — variable by effect. Corruption: tissue mineralization (Silver-Hand precedent), electromagnetic sensitivity. Usually visible.
- **Biological** — moderate ordinary cost, severe self-drain when healing others at high output. Corruption: ambient biological effects (plants wilt or flourish near the user), sensory overload. Subtle to moderate.
- **Temporal/Spatial** — subjective time cost, cognitive effort. Corruption: temporal disorientation, position uncertainty. Variable.
- **Eldritch** — low direct cost, high indirect cost (entity attention). Corruption: rapid and visible transformation. Increasingly visible.
- **Auratic** — continuous low-grade cost. Corruption: personality fixation toward the field's effect (a trust-field user becomes unable to distrust; a calm-field user becomes unable to feel urgency). Invisible cause, visible effect in the user's personality over years.

A narrow-path user at age 60 has predictable, profound, coherent corruption — Preston's loss of fear extrapolated. A multi-domain user at 60 has multiple medium-intensity alterations that do not cohere into a single pattern. **Narrow-path corruption is worse in intensity; multi-domain corruption is worse in complexity.**

## Interactions

Powers interact in observable ways.

**Kinetic cancels kinetic.** Two kinetic users pushing at each other summate (roughly linearly) as opposing vectors. A T5 kinetic meets a T4 kinetic and wins by a tier's margin. The physics is not quite Newtonian but approximately so at engagement scales.

**Auratic suppresses through effect, not force.** A trust field in a room makes lies harder but does not prevent a liar from lying; a calm field suppresses panic but does not stop a calm person from striking. Auratic effects do not cancel each other — two overlapping zones produce mixed effects (Beatrice Amar's truth-field overlapping with a visitor's deception field produces a room where neither effect fully manifests; both users feel their capacity partially blocked).

**Perceptual defends against perceptual.** A high-Perceptual user is harder to telepath, harder to illusion-trick, harder to mental-attack. The defense is reactive rather than absolute — a T6 Perceptual is still vulnerable to a T8 Perceptual attack, but the gap needed is larger than in other domains.

**Matter and Biological can conflict.** A matter-user reshaping stone near a biokinetic healer working on a patient can disrupt the biological work. Matter-healing crosses are contested — some medical biokinetics work with matter-users as teams; others find the interaction disruptive.

**Temporal contamination zones.** Sustained Temporal/Spatial work in a location produces zones where clocks disagree, echoes appear, subjective time fluctuates. Most Temporal users avoid high-duration work in populated zones.

**Eldritch and other domains are often exclusionary.** A user who accepts an Eldritch pact often finds other domains they previously accessed become less reliable. Some Eldritch-transformed individuals retain non-Eldritch capacities; many do not.

**Multi-domain users whose domains do not conflict are often more effective than single-domain higher-tier users at equivalent situational assessment.** This is the argument for integrated profiles and the reason Dr. Okafor (T7 three-domain) can out-administer some T8 narrow specialists in her domain of work.

## Power-Integrated Technology

Post-Onset technology is power-integrated. Pre-Onset electronic technology does not function — the Onset's EMP effect appears to have been permanent in some way, though the mechanism is unclear. Modern batteries do not work reliably; modern sensors fail.

**Matter-manipulation-supported fabrication** produces items at higher precision than pre-Onset mass manufacturing. A Species C watch is calibrated better than a 2022 Swiss watch; a Bear-House containment wall holds tolerances no poured concrete would match.

**Biokinetic agriculture** is the central power-integrated technology of the post-Onset economy. A T3 biokinetic can increase yield roughly 3× over baseline; saturation-yield techniques (requiring T4+) push past 8× on favorable land. The multiplier is the reason the mid-Atlantic stopped starving. The agricultural biokinetic multiplier is ~8.13× baseline at current diffusion saturation.

**Matter-manipulated ammunition reloading** keeps pre-Onset firearms functional. The powder and primer supply is biokinetically cultivated (nitrate-producing crops, sulfur extraction from biokinetic soil); shells are reshaped from salvage. Full magazines are rare and expensive; single-shot and bolt-action are standard.

**Peekskill reactor operation** is a matter-manipulation-supported hybrid — pre-Onset fission cores with matter-manipulated containment, thermal management, and electrical distribution. It is the only functional electrical grid in the mid-Atlantic, and it requires constant matter-specialist maintenance.

**Pre-Onset mechanism restoration** is a Species C specialty. Watches, small machines, mechanical computers — these can be restored by skilled C artisans and priced accordingly.

**Telepathic courier networks** (Queens Commonage Voices; Species I kin-recognition) replace pre-Onset communication infrastructure at urban scale. They are slower than radio but faster than horse-courier.

What does not exist: aircraft, functional computers, internet, cellular, satellite imagery, mechanized agriculture at pre-Onset scale, modern surgery, pharmaceutical manufacture at scale (pre-Onset stockpiles are dwindling). The post-Onset economy is preindustrial-with-manifestation, not futuristic.
