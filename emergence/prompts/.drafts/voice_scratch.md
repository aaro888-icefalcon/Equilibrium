# Voice Signature Checklist (authoring scratchpad)

Nine idiosyncratic markers the preamble must demonstrate and require:

1. "It's not X. It's Y." negative-definition pivot (≥3 in samples)
2. "Then," as beat hinge
3. Fragment-paragraph emphasis ("Opportunity.", "Time.", "Thud. Thud.")
4. Scene-capping aphorism (compressed single-line paragraph at section end)
5. Bold/italic typography load (key nouns/verbs emphasized typographically)
6. UI/diegesis interleaving (system labels flush against prose, not ironic)
7. Em-dash appositive specification ("the hum of the FDR—the cars, the engines—cuts out")
8. Specific proper-noun concreteness (FDR, NYU Langone, Kips Bay, West 23rd, First Avenue)
9. Precise-technical noun + pulp verb adjacency ("blade bites deep, bypassing the thick hide")

Two-sentence recognizability test:
(a) second-person present tense
(b) short declarative + negative-definition pivot
(c) precise-technical noun adjacent to pulp verb
