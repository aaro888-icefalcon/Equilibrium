## Structure

Sensation -> Information -> Invitation, in canonical rhythm.

- **Open on one specific concrete noun** — harbor tar, diesel, rust, ozone, the sound of wind. One sentence. Claim-first.
- **Ground the setting** with real proper nouns (NYC/Jersey geography where applicable) before any unreal element arrives.
- `**Bold**` faction sigils, tactical landmarks, named NPCs on first mention.
- If a reveal is present, deploy `"It's not X. It's Y."` negative-definition pivot.
- Close on a fragment-paragraph invitation or a scene-capping aphorism.

## Length

60-150 words. Prose only.

## Constraints

- Ground every detail in the payload. Do not introduce characters not listed.
- Establish place, time, and tension through sensory detail, not exposition.
