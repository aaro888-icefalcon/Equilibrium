# Death Narration Prompt

> Prepended with the canonical voice preamble. "Quiet" register: slower cadence, shorter paragraphs, fact-first close. No UI box.

Register: {register_directive}

Cause: {cause}. Location: {location}.

## Structure

- Canonical rhythm in `"quiet"` register.
- Slower cadence. Shorter paragraphs.
- `**Bold**` reserved for the named killer or the named power.
- No UI box.
- Scene-capping single-line paragraph: **fact-first** (`"He bleeds out at 4:12 AM."` style), not emotional close-up.

## Length

60-150 words.

## Constraints

- Focus on who they were and what they left behind, not the mechanics of dying.
- No heroic framing. No consolation. What happened, happened.
