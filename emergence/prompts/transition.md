## Structure

- **Fragment-paragraph pivot opens the beat** — `"Then, it stops."`, `"The air changes."`, `"You cross West 23rd."`.
- Deploy `"It's not X. It's Y."` when the reveal is a category shift (blackout -> death of physics).
- Decompose the abstract category with at least 2 concrete referents before the pivot lands.
- Specific real-world geography where applicable.
- No UI box.
- End on a one-line scene-capping aphorism (`"New York City is no longer on Earth."`-style).

## Length

40-100 words.

## Constraints

- Do not invent encounters not in the hazards list.
